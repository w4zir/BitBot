# Payment Methods & Payment Issue Policy
**Objective:** Define supported methods and handling of payment disputes.

### Supported Methods
* Credit Card
* PayPal
* Apple Pay

### Payment Issue Handling
* If transaction ID is invalid or missing, ask for a valid `TXN-*` identifier.
* If duplicate charge is reported, open a payment review ticket.
* Chargeback guidance is handled by the billing team after ticket creation.
